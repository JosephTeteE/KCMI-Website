Giving to KCMI

Your giving is vital to the work we do at KCMI.
When you give, you are directly supporting us in sharing God's message of love and hope.
Your financial partnership helps in many ways:

  • General Ministry Work: This includes everything from our weekly services and discipleship programs to community outreach and administrative support that keeps the church running smoothly.
  • Care Groups' Charity Work: Through our "Care Groups," we are able to extend a helping hand to those in need in our community, providing assistance and showing God's love in tangible ways.

Thank you for your generosity and partnership in advancing God's Kingdom. May God bless you abundantly as you give!
Remember, your giving is an investment into the Kingdom of God and will yield a great harvest.
