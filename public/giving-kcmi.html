Giving

Your giving is a reflection of your heart. It is an act of worship and a way to honor God with your finances. Your generosity helps us to reach more people with the message of hope and love.

We believe in the principle of sowing and reaping. When you give generously, you will reap generously. Your giving is an investment into the Kingdom of God and will yield a great harvest.

We encourage you to give cheerfully and with a heart of gratitude. Your gift will make a difference in the lives of many people.
