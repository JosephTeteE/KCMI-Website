How to Register for the Camp

Welcome to the KCMI Youth Camp 2025 Registration! To ensure a smooth process, please follow these simple steps carefully. We're excited to have you join us for a transformative experience!

Important Note

This page requires you to first make your payment to the designated account and then upload your payment receipt as part of the registration form below.

Your Registration Steps:
  • 1
    Review Camp Details & Fees:

    Please take a moment to read the important camp dates, theme, and especially the registration fee of ₦30,000 per person. All relevant information is provided just below this section.

  • 2
    Make Your Payment:

    Transfer the registration fee (₦30,000 per person) to the bank account provided below. Ensure you save or screenshot your payment confirmation/receipt.

    Account Name: Eke Orji Eke

    Bank: Access Bank

    Account Number: 0097279229

    Copied!
  • 3
    Fill Out the Registration Form:

    Complete all the required fields in the registration form located further down this page. Make sure all information is accurate.

  • 4
    Upload Your Payment Receipt:

    On the form, there will be a section to upload the payment receipt you obtained in Step 2. This is mandatory for your registration to be complete. Accepted formats are JPG, PNG, GIF, or PDF (max 5MB).

  • 5
    Submit Your Registration:

    Once all fields are filled and your receipt is uploaded, simply click the "Register for Camp" button at the bottom of the form.

  • 6
    Check Your Email:

    After successful submission, you will receive a confirmation email with your unique Submission ID and a link to your uploaded receipt. Please keep this email for your records.
